Igenous Rocks are formed from volcanic processes. Minerals of the rock are often crystallized and fused together with no surrounding cement. Sedimentary rocks are formed by deposits. They generally have a layered appearance and are weathered. The particles align themselves horizontal to the pressure. Metamorphic rock is formed as a result of changes in heat and pressure. Common in mountainous regions. They appear twisted, folded, or swirled as though altered.
